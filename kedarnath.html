Overview

The Kedarnath tour offers a spiritual journey to one of the holiest sites for Hindus. Nestled in the Himalayas, Kedarnath is known for its ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. The tour typically involves a trek of 18 kilometers, with options for horseback or helicopter transport. Pilgrims visit the temple to seek blessings and pay homage to Lord Shiva. The trek offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan landscape, adding a spiritual dimension to the journey. Kedarnath is part of the Chota Char Dham Yatra, a pilgrimage circuit in the Indian Himalayas. The tour often starts from Guptkashi, a base for pilgrims visiting Kedarnath. The journey to Kedarnath is not just a physical trek but also a spiritual and emotional experience. Many believe that visiting Kedarnath helps cleanse the soul and brings peace and fulfillment. Overall, the Kedarnath tour is a profound and transformative experience for devotees and nature enthusiasts alike.

Itinerary


DAY 01 Nainital - Guptkashi
Our driver will pick up the guest , Drive to Guptkashi view Devpriyag & Ruderpriyag on the way and then in the evening checkin the Hotel & Overnight stay.
DAY 02: Guptkashi - Kedarnath - Guptakshi (30km Drive 18km Trek / 6-7hr ) Height 3384mts
Begin your day early at 4 am and embark on your journey to the revered Kedarnath Temple. You have multiple options to cover the 18km trek, including by Horse, Palki. Arrive at the Kedarnath Temple in the afternoon for darshan, and then return to Guptkashi. Check into the hotel for an overnight stay.
DAY 03: Guptkashi - Nainital
Drop at location in Nainital.
